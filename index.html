What is WUNDER and why do you need it?

1
Thunder Tokens are the native token on ThunderCore. These tokens do not fully conform to ERC20 token standards, which prevents them from being traded directly for other ERC20 tokens.
2
The way to fix that issue is to swap "Thunder Tokens (TT)" for "Wrapped Thunder Tokens (WUNDER). These tokens conform to ERC20 standards, they also represent a 1:1 value of TT and can be swapped back for Thunder Tokens at any time.
3
Now that you have ERC20 compatible WUNDER tokens. They can be used on DEX's, Markets & other dApps throughout the ThunderCore ecosystem. At any time you can unwrap your WUNDER back to TT.

Wrap TT

When you wrap "Thunder Tokens (TT)" you'll receive "Wrapped Thunder Tokens (WUNDER)" in return. They are swapped at a 1:1 ratio and will be in your wallet immediately after the transaction is confirmed.
